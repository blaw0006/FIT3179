Response of Malaysian states to the first wave of COVID-19

In late February, 2021, Malaysia began its national immunisation programme in an attempt to reduce the severity of the forecased waves of COVID-19. This visualisation examines the efficiency and speed with which different Malaysian states adminisitered vaccines to its population over the first month of the programme.
